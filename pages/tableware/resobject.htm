ABOUT RES OBJECTS

The material we work with — Porcelain — is a very interesting material, which is physically not so fragile, but it is quite easy to "offend". Porcelain production is not suited to speed, nervousness or anger. We have learned to work with it, but this was not always the case.

Our mission is to show the boundless possibilities of porcelain. Although in Russia it is considered a conservative material, every day we discover new facets and possibilities, and it never ceases to amaze.

These products are inspired by architecture, new technologies, and a mixture of other multidisciplinary areas. Recently we found inspiration in glimpses of beauty, where there are usually none.

Every year we release 1−2 new collections of our own design from our workshop: tableware, jewelry and accessories, Christmas decorations.
