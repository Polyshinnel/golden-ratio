ABOUT LE COQ PORCELAINE

LE COQ PORCELAINE’ s project comes to reality in 2019, when the brand is acquired by ILSA s.r.l. From here the company embarks on a new itinerary of designing and developing high performance products intended for the Ho.Re.Ca. and banqueting world. The brand was created during the 80 ’ s in Florence, the beating heart of the Italian culture, cradle of the Renaissance and one of the most exquisite art cities in the world.

The acquisition of the brand comes as more than a business takeover: it sets off the union of two realities. On one side the Tuscan artistic genius - embodied in the resourcefulness and vitality of the brand LE COQ PORCELAINE - on the other, the Piedmontese reliability and entrepreneurial attitude - represented by Ilsa.

After more than half century of activity, ILSA keeps on investing and putting its energy and resources at the disposal of the hospitality professional and with the LE COQ PORCELAINE assortment the company aims at expanding the potential of its offering. LE COQ PORCELAINE assortment, in fact, offers elegant and innovative tableware and buffet solutions to the Ho.Re.Ca. professionals with its marvellous Porcelain, Stoneware, Bone China, Alumina Porcelain and New Bone China products

Our work on unique designs and shapes and the research for highquality materials are aimed at obtaining both high performance and unparalleled aesthetic features for all our products. LE COQ PORCELAINE brand grants high quality, resistance and strength of any product meant for the Ho.Re.Ca. market. The items of our collections are strictly tested and controlled according to Ilsa quality standard as to meet the necessary requirements to come in contact with food and resist to acid substances and water absorption.
