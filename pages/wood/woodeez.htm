ABOUT US

Woodeez — is a Russian brand located in Moscow city. Company was founded by two craftsmen — Stanislav Buckovsky and Viktor Butko. Over the years we have created our own technology for manufacturing home accessories from oak wood that guarantees a premium quality and life lasting durability of our products.

Today, our company produces kitchen accessories, cutlery, boards bowls and dishes that have a unique handcrafted texture for serving, on one side and smooth surface for cutting on the other.

HAND-CRAFTED

All our products are hand-made and undergo a five-step quality control process.

THE ENVIRONMENT

During production we only use natural materials and edible oils.

UNIQUE

Wood is a natural material and every time the texture is therefore unique.

CUSTOMISATION

If required we can change colours, form and size, or engrave words, phrases or logos.
