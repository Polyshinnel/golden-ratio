CONCRETIKA — MADE WITH LOVE

We produce flowerpots and planters, decor items for home and garden, small architectural forms and much, much more. We have twice received a gold medal at the largest international landscape festival — MOSFLOWERSHOW.

We work with concrete, cast iron, metal, wood and fiberglass, and are constantly innovating, experimenting, learning and developing. The most important thing for us is that we get great pleasure from our work — which is why we do it with love!

CONCRETIKA was founded in 2015. From a small studio workshop consisting of two people, we grew into a small, but already fully-fledged production enterprise. Today, our company employs more than 40 people.

In addition to our standard products, we produce custom-made planters to individual designs. We can make flowerpots according to sketches and drawings, choose a texture or paint according to RAL.
