ABOUT ILSA

ILSA s.r.l. is an international brand leader in the manufacture and supply of professional items for the Ho.Re.Ca and hospitality industry. The company was founded in Turin as a factory for laminating and molding aluminum. In more than 75 years of experience Ilsa has developed a full proposal of kitchenware, barware and tableware: more than 3000 products designed and manufactured to last over time.

The first item produced with the brand ILSA is the iconic Napoletana drip-over coffee maker. At the beginning of the 70's, the first stainless steel items are produced and, in this branch, the company estabilishes itself as a well-known hollowware manufacturer. With the introduction of the first in cast-iron products, the Dietella series was launched: today this line of cast-iron grill pans is still among the best sellers items of the company.

ILSA focuses on research and product development, bringing the assortment to multiply exponentially over the span of twenty years. Nowadays, beside marketing more than 3000 products all over the world, including professional knives designed and manufactured in collaboration with the Turkish company Pirge, ILSA is the exclusive distributor for the Italian market of the Valencian paellere of the Garcima brand and of Efay melamine tableware and buffet products.

After more than half century of activity, ILSA keeps on investing and putting its energy and resources at the disposal of the hospitality professionals. With Le Coq Porcelaine assortment the company aims at expanding the potential of its offering. In 2019 with the acquisition of the brand, in fact, the company embarks on a new journey in the professional porcelain tableware sector, by designing and developing high performance products intended for the Ho.Re.Ca. and banqueting world.
